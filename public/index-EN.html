Everything for your events


MoonSpace is a multifunctional venue located in Milan's Design District. It is a bright and white space with independent access through a green area, consisting of 160 sqm on two levels. The location is suitable for hosting business or private events, temporary showrooms, photo shoots, product presentations, training sessions, press conferences, and private parties. It has been recovered from an early 20th-century industrial space and preserves the soul of Italian design and Milanese courtyards, but affirms a strong international spirit. The location consists of three communicating rooms, the central one with double height, a bathroom with double services, and a fully equipped kitchen for food preparation or catering use.
